    Bronze-Silver-Gold layers and the lakehouse pattern.

    Raw ingestion, landing zones, and immutable source copies.

    Full load, incremental, CDC, and streaming ingestion.

  4. Silver Layer

    Cleansed, conformed, deduplicated, and typed data.

  5. Silver Transformations

    Data quality rules, standardization, and enrichment.

  6. Gold Layer

    Business aggregates, KPIs, and serving-layer tables.

  7. Gold Data Modeling

    Star schema, wide tables, and semantic layer design.

  8. Delta Lake in Medallion

    ACID guarantees across layers and MERGE for CDC.

  9. Unity Catalog Governance

    Catalog structure, permissions, and lineage across layers.

  10. Streaming Medallion

    Real-time bronze-to-gold with Structured Streaming.

  11. Testing Medallion Pipelines

    Data quality checks, Great Expectations, and assertions.

  12. Monitoring & Lineage

    Pipeline observability and data lineage tracking.

  13. Migration to Medallion

    Transitioning from legacy warehouses to medallion.

  14. Cost Optimization

    Storage tiering, compaction, and compute efficiency per layer.

  15. Medallion Capstone

    Implement bronze-silver-gold on Azure Databricks.
