Path Phases

Phase 1: AI Foundations

History, paradigms, problem solving, BFS/DFS, heuristic search, game trees.

Phase 2: Knowledge & Reasoning

KR, propositional/FOL logic, expert systems, planning, uncertainty, symbolic NLP.

Phase 3: Modern Context

Classic CV, robotics, ethics, modern landscape, AI/ML/DL distinctions, agents, trends.

Prerequisites

  • Basic Python
  • Comfort with graphs and set notation

What You Will Achieve

  • ✓ Implement core search and game algorithms
  • ✓ Model knowledge with logic and planning
  • ✓ Connect classical AI to contemporary systems

All Topics

  1. History of AI

    AI milestones from Turing to modern deep learning systems.

  2. AI Paradigms

    Symbolic AI, connectionism, and hybrid approaches.

  3. Problem Solving

    State spaces, goal states, and formulation of AI problems.

  4. BFS & DFS

    Uninformed search strategies and completeness properties.

  5. Heuristic Search

    Greedy best-first and A* algorithm fundamentals.

  6. Game Trees & Minimax

    Adversarial search, minimax, and alpha-beta pruning.

  7. Knowledge Representation

    Facts, rules, frames, and semantic networks.

  8. Propositional Logic

    AND, OR, NOT, implications, and inference rules.

  9. First-Order Logic

    Predicates, quantifiers, and knowledge base reasoning.

  10. Expert Systems

    Rule-based systems, inference engines, and MYCIN-style design.

  11. Automated Planning

    STRIPS, plan spaces, and classical planning algorithms.

  12. Reasoning Under Uncertainty

    Bayesian networks and probabilistic reasoning.

  13. Symbolic NLP

    Parsing, grammars, and early language understanding.

  14. Classic Computer Vision

    Edge detection, HOG, and pre-deep-learning CV.

  15. Robotics Introduction

    Sensors, actuators, and sense-plan-act loops.

  16. AI Ethics

    Alignment, transparency, and societal impact of AI systems.

  17. Modern AI Landscape

    Deep learning, LLMs, and the current AI stack.

  18. AI vs ML vs DL

    Clarifying terminology and scope of each field.

  19. Intelligent Agents

    PEAS framework, agent types, and environments.

  20. Future Trends

    Multimodal AI, embodied AI, and research frontiers.
